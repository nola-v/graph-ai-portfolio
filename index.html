About Me

Hello! My name is Nola and I enjoy transforming complex health data into actionable insights. My journey in public health data started over 10 years ago, and I've developed a deep understanding of Pacific health systems along the way.

Currently, I am the Data and Reporting Officer (NCD) at the Pacific Community (SPC) in Suva, Fiji. My main focus is on database development, data visualisation, and strengthening national NCD data capacity through technical support and training.

I hold an MSc in Computing and Information Systems and an MPH in Biostatistics. I am constantly exploring new technologies, including Generative AI, to enhance reporting methodologies and data accuracy.
